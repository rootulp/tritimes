## Project Overview

TriTimes is a web application that lets athletes view their historical IronMan and IronMan 70.3 triathlon results. For each result, it shows statistical distributions for each discipline (swim, bike, run) and total time, comparing performance against the athlete's age group and overall field.

Data is collected from https://www.ironman.com/races/im703-new-york/results. Inspired by https://www.hyresult.com/.
